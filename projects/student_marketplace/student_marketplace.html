description

Nexus is a custom-built classified ads platform designed exclusively for TMU students. It offers an easy-to-use interface where students can post, explore, and filter items for sale or wanted. The platform also includes features such as a dashboard for managing posts, academic services like textbook exchanges, and study group listings to enhance the TMU community's academic and social experience.
